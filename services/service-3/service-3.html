Talent Out Reach Program

Our E Learning program is an Online training program focused towards providing learning & development opportunities to  Budding staffing professionals, young college graduates, War veterans, Women wanting to get back to work or anyone wanting to make a career in US IT staffing or anyone just wanting to learn about what happens in this space.

The program is driven directly by our founder utilizing & sharing his three decades of US Staffing experience, a time spent playing on the ground in both Sales & Recruiting functions along with several leadership roles played in his Staffing journey, Leading P&L of over $60 mil USD run rate annually & successfully managing practices up to  350 employees 

As an organization we also have several tie ups with faculties, Trainers, guest trainers & Industry leaders associated with our program. Our course curriculum is quite comprehensive in nature for both Sales & Recruiting professionals at a nominal price with a mix of online classes / sessions followed up with Assessments to test the knowledge of the user & providing the user guidance on areas where he / she might have to practice more. As a team –we are proud that our training program has so many members who saw the benefits either in their job interviews or helped them by brushing up & fine tuning their skills & improve their performance in their current jobs. Our training program also gives the users the freedom to cherry pick a curriculum that interests them & meets their professional needs.

Simply schedule an e-meeting with our team to learn more about our offering in this space & pick a course that interests you & might be the best fit for you
